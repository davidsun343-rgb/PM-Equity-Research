# Philip Morris International — Equity Research Report

## Investment Thesis
**BUY | Price Target: $229 | ~38% Upside to $166 (March 2026)**

A full-scope equity research report and DCF valuation model for Philip Morris International (NYSE: PM), 
built to sell-side standards. The model supports a bullish thesis driven by PMI's accelerating 
smoke-free transition, IQOS pricing power, and durable free cash flow generation.
